Introduction to FewNLU

The Few-shot Learning for Natural Language Understanding (FewNLU) project is a collection of toolkits and resources for training, evaluating, and analyzing few-shot natural language understanding systems. FewNLU consists of:

1. A systematic toolkit for few-shot natural language understanding. It integrates commonly-used NLU tasks, advanced few-shot learning methods, and supports an experimentally proven effective few-shot setting.
2. A benchmark consisting of seven few-shot natural language understanding tasks built on existing FewGLUE datasets. Additional small-size development set is built for the purpose of hyper-parameter & model selection.
3. A public leaderboard for tracking performance on the FewNLU benchmark.

Base on FewNLU, we have conducted systematic studies in terms of several fundamental problems in the field of few-shot natural language understanding, including few-shot setting and legal re-evaluation of existing methods, and have achieved the state-of-the-arts on the FewGLUE datasets. Please refer to our paper (https://github.com/zheng-yanan/FewNLU-prep) for more details. The ultimate goal of FewNLU is to drive the research in the development of effective and robust few-shot natural language understanding systems.

Data Download

  • Recommended Few-shot Setting

    The benchmark dataset supports our recommended legal & effective few-shot learning by providing an additional small-size development set of 32 examples.

  • Diverse & Complicated NLU Tasks

    The tasks are complicated sentence-pair or sentence-triple tasks that demand advanced understanding capabilities. Besides, the tasks are diverse, ranging from question answering, textual entailment, co-reference resolution, causal reasoning to word sense disambiguation.

  • Support Integrating Other Learning Paradigms

    The benchmark dataset also supports research that integrates other learning paradigms (such as semi-supervised learning) with few-shot learning by providing additional unlabeled datasets.

Toolkit Installation & Usage

FewNLU provides systematic few-shot learning toolkits for natural language understanding tasks, which integrates commonly-used tasks and advanced few-shot learning methods. FewNLU supports:

1. Reasonable and effective few-shot setting which uses a small-size dev set to perform model and hyper-param selection.
2. Different types of formalization for few-shot learning tasks, including standard sequence classification form, cloze form with hand-crafted prompts, and cloze form with continuous prompts.
3. Multiple advanced methods that could boost the performance of few-shot NLU tasks, e.g., PET, ADAPET, P-tuning, self-training, multi-task learning etc.
4. Easy-to-use interfaces that allow either directly accessing existing few-shot NLU tasks and methods, or quickly customizing new ones.

Please refer to the documents (https://github.com/zheng-yanan/FewNLU-prep/blob/main/README.md) for detailed instructions of FewNLU usages.
